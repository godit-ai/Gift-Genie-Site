Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you visit giftgenie.info, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address: If you voluntarily sign up for our waitlist or contact us, we collect your email address to respond and send launch updates.
  • Usage data: We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This may include pages visited, time on site, referral source, browser type, and device information.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies: Our site and third-party partners (including affiliate networks) may place cookies on your browser. See the Cookies section below for details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate with you about Gift Genie (e.g., launch notifications if you join the waitlist)
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To understand how visitors use and navigate our site
  • To deliver relevant affiliate product links and track referral commissions

Cookies and Tracking

Our site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymized data about site usage. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: When you click an affiliate link on our site (such as links to Amazon, Walmart, or other retailers), the retailer or affiliate network may place a cookie on your browser to track the referral. This is how we earn commissions on qualifying purchases. These cookies are set by the retailer or affiliate network, not by Gift Genie directly.
  • Essential cookies: We may use basic cookies to remember your preferences on our site.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features.

Third-Party Services

We may use or link to the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics (analytics and usage tracking)
  • Amazon Associates (affiliate links and tracking)
  • Impact / Walmart affiliate program (affiliate links and tracking)
  • Formspree (waitlist form submissions)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated analytics data to improve our services. If you click an affiliate link, the destination retailer will handle your data under their own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your email address only as long as needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected (e.g., waitlist notifications). You can request removal at any time by emailing support@giftgenie.info.

Children's Privacy

Gift Genie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please email support@giftgenie.info.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@giftgenie.info.
