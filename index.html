FAQ

Quick answers. Easy start.

Is Gift Genie free?

Yes — Gift Genie is free to download and use. The AI gift generator, contact import, reminders, and Amazon shopping links are all included at no cost.

How does the AI gift generator work?

Tell the Genie who you're shopping for, pick an occasion and budget, then describe what they're into. The AI asks a few smart follow-up questions to narrow things down, then generates a personalized list of gift ideas you can browse, save, or buy directly on Amazon.

When will it be available on the App Store?

Very soon — the app is built and in final testing for iOS and Android. Join the waitlist to get notified the moment it goes live.

Can I import my contacts?

Yes — import contacts from your phone with one tap. You can add notes about each person's interests, sizes, and favorites so the AI gives better suggestions over time.

Do you send reminders for birthdays and holidays?

Absolutely. Add important dates and Gift Genie sends push notifications ahead of time so you're never caught off guard. You can customize how far in advance you're reminded.

Can I save gifts and buy them later?

Yes — save any gift idea to your list or tap the heart to favorite it. When you're ready, open the Amazon link directly from the app to check reviews and purchase.
