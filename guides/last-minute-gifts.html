Best Last-Minute Gifts

Time is running out but you still want to give something great. These gifts either arrive instantly (digital delivery), ship within 1-2 days, or can be picked up locally. No one needs to know you waited until the last minute.

  • E-Gift Card Delivered instantly to their inbox. Choose their favorite store, restaurant, or streaming service. Simple, effective, and always appreciated.
  • Online Experience or Class A virtual cooking class, art workshop, or wine tasting can be booked and gifted immediately. Many platforms offer instant gift certificates.
  • Digital Magazine or Newspaper Subscription An instant-access subscription to a publication they enjoy. Starts immediately and keeps giving for months.
  • Audiobook or Ebook Credit Gift credits for audiobook or ebook platforms. They arrive instantly and let the recipient choose exactly what they want to read.
  • Food Delivery Gift Card A gift card for a food delivery service gives them a free meal (or several) whenever they want. Universally useful and instantly delivered.
  • Streaming Service Subscription Gift a few months of a music, TV, or movie streaming service. Sets up in minutes and provides immediate entertainment.
  • Flower or Plant Delivery Most local and national florists offer same-day or next-day delivery. A bouquet or potted plant is a classic gift that works for almost any occasion.
  • Charity Donation in Their Name For someone who has everything, a donation to a cause they care about is meaningful and instant. Many charities offer printable gift certificates.
  • Same-Day Pickup Items Most major retailers offer same-day in-store or curbside pickup. Order online, grab it on your way, and you've got a wrapped gift in hand within hours.
  • Handwritten Letter or Coupon Book Sometimes the most meaningful gift costs nothing. A heartfelt letter or a homemade book of "coupons" (breakfast in bed, a movie night of their choice) shows real thought.

How We Picked These

Speed was the top priority, but we didn't sacrifice quality. Every pick on this list can be secured within 24 hours and still feels thoughtful. We emphasized digital and instant-delivery options that genuinely delight. See our Editorial Policy.
