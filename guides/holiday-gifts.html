Best Holiday Gifts

The holiday season means shopping for lots of people at once. Whether you celebrate Christmas, Hanukkah, or another winter holiday, these gift ideas cover a wide range of recipients and budgets so you can check everyone off your list.

  • Cozy Blanket and Hot Chocolate Set A soft throw blanket bundled with gourmet hot chocolate mix makes a warm, festive gift that's perfect for cold winter evenings.
  • Wireless Charging Pad A sleek wireless charger declutters the nightstand and keeps devices topped up. A universal tech gift that works for almost anyone.
  • Cookbook from a Favorite Chef A beautifully photographed cookbook inspires new recipes and looks great on a kitchen shelf. Match the cuisine to the recipient's tastes.
  • Premium Socks or Loungewear Set High-quality socks, pajama sets, or loungewear are holiday classics for a reason. Everyone loves receiving something impossibly soft to wear at home.
  • Portable Espresso Maker A compact, manual espresso maker lets coffee lovers brew quality espresso anywhere — at work, while camping, or traveling.
  • Puzzle (1000 pieces) A beautiful 1000-piece puzzle is a perfect holiday activity. Great for families or anyone who enjoys a screen-free challenge during the holidays.
  • Personalized Ornament A custom ornament commemorating a milestone (new baby, new home, engagement) becomes a cherished annual tradition piece.
  • Electric Hand Warmer A rechargeable hand warmer fits in a jacket pocket and keeps hands toasty during winter walks, sporting events, and commutes.
  • Gourmet Food Gift Basket A curated basket of specialty snacks, jams, nuts, and treats feels festive and indulgent. Choose local artisan products for extra character.
  • Reading Light or Book Lamp A clip-on reading light with adjustable brightness is ideal for readers who like to wind down before bed without disturbing a partner.
  • Donation to Charity Gift Card Give them the choice of directing a donation to a charity they care about. Meaningful, impactful, and perfect for those who have everything.
  • Board Game or Card Game A new game for family game night is a holiday tradition in many households. Choose something that scales well from 2 to 6+ players.
