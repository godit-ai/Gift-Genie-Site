About Gift Genie

We believe the best gifts come from paying attention. Gift Genie helps you do exactly that.

Our Mission

Gift Genie was created out of a simple frustration: you hear someone mention something they love, and by the time their birthday rolls around, you've completely forgotten. We built Gift Genie to solve that problem — a simple app that lets you capture gift ideas the moment they come to you, organize them by person, and get reminded when it matters.

Our goal is to take the stress out of gift giving. We want every gift to feel personal, thoughtful, and on time — without the last-minute scramble.

What We Do

Gift Genie is an iOS app (coming soon) and website that helps you:

  • Save gift ideas for the people you care about as soon as inspiration strikes
  • Organize ideas with notes, links, sizes, and price ranges
  • Track birthdays, holidays, anniversaries, and other important dates
  • Get smart reminders so you're never caught off guard
  • Browse curated gift guides when you need a little inspiration

Our Approach to Recommendations

When we recommend products or link to retailers, we aim to be genuinely helpful. Our gift guides and suggestions are based on real research into what people actually enjoy receiving. We may earn a small commission from affiliate links (at no extra cost to you), which helps keep Gift Genie running. We always prioritize honest recommendations over affiliate revenue. Who We Are

Gift Genie is a small, independent team passionate about making everyday life a little more thoughtful. We're based in the US and focused on building tools that help people show they care — one gift at a time.
